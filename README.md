# T20-Men-s-Cricket-World-Cup-2022
## OverView
- The World Cup Cricket T20 2022 Analytics Project is an exploration into the statistical insights and trends surrounding one of the most exciting cricket tournaments globally.
- Leveraging the power of Power Query and Power BI, this project delves into the data from the T20 World Cup 2022 to uncover patterns, performance metrics, and player/team analyses.

# Tool Used
- Power Query: Used for data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) from various data sources.
- Power BI: Employed for data modeling, visualization, and interactive reporting.

# Data Sources
- Data sourced from official T20 World Cup 2022 statistics.

# Condition for selecting players in Every Position
## Openers/Power Hitters(2 Players)
- Batting AVG. > 30
- Strike Rate > 140
- Innings Batted > 3
- Boundry % > 50
- Batting Position < 4

## Anchors/Middle orders (3 players)
- Batting AVG. > 40
- Strike Rate > 125
- Innings Batted > 3
- Avg.Balls Faced > 20
- Batting Position > 2

## Finisher/ Lower Order Anchor(1 player)
- Batting AVG. > 25
- Strike Rate > 130
- Innings Batted > 3
- Avg.Balls Faced > 12
- Batting Position > 4
- Innings Bowled > 1

## Finisher/ Lower Order Anchor(2 player)
- Batting AVG. > 15
- Strike Rate > 140
- Innings Batted > 2
- Batting Position > 4
- Innings Bowled > 2
- Bowling Economy < 7
- Bowling Strike Rate < 20

## Specialist Fast Bowlers(3 player)
- Innings Bowled > 4
- Bowling Economy < 7
- Bowling strike Rate < 16
- Bowling Style = "Fast"
- Bowling Average < 20
- Dot Ball % > 40
